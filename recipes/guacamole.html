Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip used in Mexican and American cuisines. It is simple to make and can me used in a variety of ways, including as a dip, spread, or condiment.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 diced jalapeno
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and mash avocados in a bowl until smooth and creamy. Remove seeds.
  2. Chop ingredients
  3. Mix all ingredients into bowl with avocados and stir until evenly mixed
  4. Enjoy!
