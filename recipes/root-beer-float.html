Root Beer Float

Description

Nothing beats a cold root beer float on a hot summer day. Creamy vanilla ice cream pairs perfectly with the sweet kick of A&W root beer.

Ingredients

  • A&W Root Beer
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Whipped cream
  • Cherry (optional)

Steps

  1. Freeze a tall glass for 10 minutes.
  2. Add 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream to the chilled glass.
  3. Slowly pour root beer on top of the ice cream. Take time to allow the fizz to settle.
  4. Swirl a layer of whipped cream on top of the root beer foam. Add a cherry on top if desired.
  5. Go outside and enjoy!
