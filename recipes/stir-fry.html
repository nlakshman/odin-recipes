Stir-Fry

Description

Stir frying vegetables is a quick and easy way to make a tast and healthy dish. This method of cooking is inpired by Asian cuisine. Once prepared, these veggies can be served with rice, noodles, or as a standalone meal.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Scalions
  • Carrots
  • Broccoli
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell Peppers
  • Peas
  • Baby Corn
  • Water Chestnuts
  • Stir Fry Sauce
    • Soy Sauce
    • Vinegar
    • Sesame Oil
    • Cornstarch
    • Brown Sugar
    • Minced Garlic
    • Minced Ginger

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok
  2. Chop up ginger and garlic, then fry in oil till aromatic
  3. Add chopped veggetables and stir frequently
  4. Mix sauce ingredients in a bowl
  5. 5 minutes before vegetables are cooked, add stir fry sauce to the wok and mix in with veggetables
  6. Once veggetables are cooked, remove from heat and serve in bowl or with rice/noodles
